Senku e Shoujiki ni iu to, kimochi wo kotoba ni suru no wa amari tokui janai. Mashite ya, kimi no koto to naru to, naosara muzukashii. Demo, kimi no sekai no mikata ni wa, itsumo kanshin shiteru. Shuuchūryoku ga atte, ganko de, soshite tsuneni mirai wo mite iru. Sonna kimi no sugata wa, dare ni demo mane dekiru mono janai.

Kimi ni wa ōkina mokuhyō ga aru koto mo wakatteru. Moshikashitara, dareka no kimochi nante, ima no kimi ni wa kankei nai kamo shirenai. Dakara, nanika wo kitai shiteru wake janai.

Tada, tsutaetakatta dake. Kono konran shita sekai to tatakau kimi wo, tooku kara zutto miteru ningen ga ite, sono subete wo shinjiteru tte koto. Sore dake. — [Fara]
